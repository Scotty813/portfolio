About

Hello there! I am a web developer in the Tampa Bay area with a focus on what the client sees and interacts with. My dream is to become an expert in creating both beautiful and intuitive user interfaces, which includes building web applications as well as websites. My core skills include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and a touch of SQL and PHP. Some skills in the incubator include ReactJS and D3.


Technology has always been an interest of mine, however I recently decided to make it my career. The journey is one that never ends, with new things to learn every single day. The most rewarding aspect of this field, for me, is the overwhelming sense of accmplishment that comes with solving problems you never thought you could. That is what keeps me going, and that is why I do what I do.


I offer my skills to those who wish to put their content on the internet. No matter your idea, I am here to help! Even though I have a B.S. in Psychology, I can't read minds, so send me a message below and let's talk.
